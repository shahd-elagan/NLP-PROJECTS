Turkey is a country located at the crossroads of Europe and Asia, making it a bridge between two continents.
Its largest city is Istanbul, which is famous for its unique position spanning both Europe and Asia.

Turkey has a rich history influenced by many civilizations, including the Byzantine and Ottoman Empires. The capital city is Ankara, and the official language is Turkish.

The Turkish economy is diverse, with key sectors including manufacturing, agriculture, tourism, and trade. Tourism plays an important role, with popular destinations such as Cappadocia, Antalya, and Ephesus.

Turkey is also known for its cuisine, which includes dishes like kebabs, baklava, and Turkish tea.
